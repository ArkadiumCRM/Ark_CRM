=== CROSS-FILE FUNCTIONALITY MATRIX ===

** COMMON ACROSS ALL 14 FILES **
- Top Navigation: Tabs (Ueberblick, Briefing, Werdegang, Assessment, Jobbasket, Prozesse, History, Dokumente, Dok-Generator, Reminders)
- Topbar: Candidate-Selector (MM/PW), Status-Badge (Active), Sparte (Tiefbau, etc.), Profilvollstaendigkeit-Indicator
- Sidebar: Candidate-List with Chips and Badges
- Search-Bar: Ctrl+K trigger

** BUTTONS & ACTIONS (ACROSS FILES) **
1. Ueberblick: Bearbeiten (pencil), Speichern-Button (auto-sync)
2. Briefing: "Prufen & Bestaetigen" (AI action), "Speichern"
3. Werdegang: "Station hinzufuegen" (pill-button), Filter-Buttons (Alle, Jobs, Ausbildung)
4. JobBasket: "Vorgeschlagene Jobs", Rejection-Modals, Job-Preview-Popup
5. Prozesse: Date-Input-Modals, "Debrief" Popup, Drag-Reorder
6. History: Search-Clear, Attachment-Download, "Drawer oeffnen"
7. Dokumente: Sort-Checkboxes, Doc-Download, Version-Selector
8. Dok-Generator: Zoom+/-, Document-Generate, Preview-Zoom
9. Reminders: Snooze-Btn, Edit-Inline, New-Reminder, Priority-Filter
10. AI-Analyse: Dimension-Cards, Score-Details
11. Assessment: Status-Filter, AI-Score-View
12. Scheelen HM: Opportunity-Details, DISC-Breakdown
13. Teamrad: Comparison-Filter, Equipment-Selector, Ampel-Legend
14. Vergleich: Candidate-Compare, Gauge-Filter, Dimension-Toggle

** SPECIAL WIDGETS BY TYPE **

CHARTS & VISUALIZATION:
  - Spider/Radar Charts: JobBasket (IMP/Gehalt/Funktion), Teamrad (Comp-Radar), Vergleich (Gauges)
  - Fit-Gauges: AI-Analyse, Assessment, Scheelen (Large Opportunity-Bars)
  - Mini-Progress-Bars: Assessment, across cards
  - Traffic-Light (3-state Ampel): Teamrad (Comp-Status), Scheelen

MODALS & DRAWERS:
  - Job-Preview-Popup: JobBasket
  - Rejection-Modal: JobBasket (mit Feedback-Textfeld)
  - Date-Input-Modal: Prozesse, History
  - Debrief-Popup: Prozesse
  - Drawer-Full-Height: History, Dokumente, Scheelen

TIMELINES:
  - Vertical Timeline: Werdegang (Career-History), History (Event-Log)
  - Timeline-Entry: Card-based, with metadata (Dates, Company, Position, Tags)
  - Timeline-Dots: full/partial/empty status (Briefing Quick-Jump)

BADGES & STATUS INDICATORS:
  - Sparte-Pill: Gold-background, text (Tiefbau, etc.)
  - Status-Badges: Active, Inactive, colors
  - File-Type-Icons: Dokumente (PDF, Word, etc. colors)
  - Gate-Status-Colors: Dokumente, Prozesse
  - Priority-Dots: Reminders (red/amber/green)
  - AI-Status-Dot: Versioning indicators

FORM ELEMENTS:
  - Checkbox: Sort (Dokumente), Reminders, Gate2-Options (JobBasket)
  - Color-Chips: Version-selectors (Briefing history)
  - Filter-Buttons: Werdegang (Jobs/Ausbildung), Reminders (Priority), Status-Filters
  - Radio-Buttons: Assessment, Scheelen
  - Text-Input: Reminders (new reminder), inline-edit fields

LISTS & GRIDS:
  - Doc-List: Sortierbar (Dokumente)
  - Reminder-List: Gruppiert nach Priority, mit Checkboxes
  - Comparison-Grid: Teamrad (multi-row), Vergleich (columns)
